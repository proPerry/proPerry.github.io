About Us

We are the extreme crew that bring the most out of your turtle. If you care about your turtle at all, you'll bring it to us. We'll put it to work while you're at work.

Some turtles are more difficult than others. Here is a list of turtles that we support:

  • Sea Turtle
  • Pelomedusa
  • Carbonemys
  • Chelydridae
  • Snapping Turtles
  • And for the last time, NO BOX TURTLES.
